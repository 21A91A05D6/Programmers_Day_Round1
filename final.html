To join live chat with Doc, follow these steps:

  1. Visit our website.
  2. Create an account or log in if you already have one.
  3. Search for a doctor or specialist you want to consult with.
  4. Choose a convenient time slot for your consultation.
  5. Complete the payment process.
  6. Wait for the scheduled consultation and connect with the doctor.

The fees for patients may vary depending on the doctor and the type of consultation. It is recommended to check the fee structure on the doctor's profile before booking a consultation.

You can pay for your consultation using various payment methods, including credit/debit cards and online payment gateways. The available payment options will be displayed during the booking process.

Chat with Doc offers consultations in multiple medical departments, including but not limited to cardiology, dermatology, gastroenterology, and more. You can explore the available departments on our website.

Chat with Doc does not provide emergency medical services. In case of a medical emergency, please dial your local emergency number or visit the nearest emergency room.
